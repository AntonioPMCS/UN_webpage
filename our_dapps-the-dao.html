Unity Network Community Governance

You have a say when in deciding what financial tools Unity Network creates next. Here’s how it works.

You Vote. We Develop. You Benefit.

Decentralized autonomous organizations (DAOs) are governing bodies that are responsible for the resources of the projects they are associated with. They make big decisions for the project and are responsible for its long-term success.

This means you.

We’re working hard to bring you a DAO voting mechanism that’s simple to use and doesn't cost you a fortune in gas fees. When it’s ready, you’ll make use of special voting tokens to cast your vote on what Unity Network develops next.

Unity staff will provide its community with several decentralized application (dApp) options based on research and marketing data, and you’ll choose which gets developed. Unity Network will also provide an avenue through which community members can make their own suggestions, which will be evaluated by the team and subsequently be put to a vote, if approved. It’s the perfect combination of expert guidance and true community governance.

We look forward to working with our community to develop the next generation of dApps and protocols.
