Token Swap

Below, you can initiate the process of swapping your LOCK for UNT tokens. This swap supports the rebranding of Meridian Network to Unity Network, which you can read about here: Detailed Medium Announcement.

Instructions

  1. Log into the Metamask account in which your LOCK is stored
  2. Select "Connect" below and follow the directions within the Metamask pop-up window to connect your wallet to our swap portal.
  3. Once connected, please select "Transfer" below and follow the directions within Metamask to finalize your token swap. Your LOCK will be transferred to the swap address and you will later receive an equivalent amount of UNT tokens.

On MM/DD/YEAR, you will receive a number of UNT tokens equal to the number of LOCK tokens you've transferred today. UNT tokens will be delivered to the ERC20 wallet address from which your LOCK originated.
